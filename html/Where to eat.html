Whisper Pines Coffee
Unwind at Whispering Pines Coffee House, a cozy spot offering gourmet coffee, light bites, and a relaxed atmosphere perfect for a quick refresh or a leisurely afternoon.
Trail's End Diner
Fuel up at Trail's End Diner, a classic spot offering all-day breakfast, hearty comfort food, and a welcoming atmosphere perfect for families and travelers.
Pinecone Tavern
Enjoy a casual dining experience at Pinecone Tavern, where hearty meals, craft beers, and a laid-back vibe make it a local favorite for lunch and dinner.
Sunset Bistro
Dine in style at Sunset Bistro, where creative dishes and an elegant atmosphere meet stunning lake and mountain views. Perfect for romantic dinners or special occasions.
Mountain Hearth Café
Stop by Mountain Hearth Café for cozy comfort food, freshly baked pastries, and locally roasted coffee. A perfect spot for a quick bite or a relaxed afternoon.
The Lakeside Grille
Savor fresh, locally inspired dishes with a stunning view of Sunny Lake. The Lakeside Grille combines delicious cuisine with a relaxed lakeside ambiance perfect for any occasion.
Alpine View Restaurant
Enjoy fine dining at Alpine View Restaurant, with upscale menu options, expert service, and stunning panoramic views. Perfect for a sophisticated dining experience.
Harvest Table
Experience farm-to-table dining at Harvest Table, where locally sourced ingredients shine in creative dishes served in an elegant, welcoming atmosphere.
The Garden Grill
Dine in elegance at The Garden Grill, featuring premium steaks, fresh seafood, and an extensive wine list in a sophisticated setting with attentive service.
Fireside Café
Warm up at Fireside Café, a rustic spot with hearty soups, sandwiches, and hot drinks perfect for hikers and campers looking for comfort food after outdoor adventures.
Trailhead Grill
Refuel at Trailhead Grill, offering quick, tasty meals in a casual setting. Perfect for campers seeking hearty portions before or after a day of outdoor activities.
Campfire Kitchen
Experience rustic dining at Campfire Kitchen, where comfort food meets outdoor ambiance. Enjoy hearty meals in a casual setting perfect for hungry adventurers.
Cabin Brews & Bites
Stop by Cabin Brews & Bites for artisanal coffee, fresh pastries, and light meals in a cozy, wood-paneled setting that captures the essence of cabin life.
Woodland Eatery
Experience rustic charm at Woodland Eatery, featuring hearty, home-style meals made with local ingredients in a warm, cabin-inspired setting.
Pine Lodge Dining
Savor comfort food with a gourmet twist at Pine Lodge Dining, where cabin-inspired decor meets innovative cuisine in a relaxed, welcoming environment.
